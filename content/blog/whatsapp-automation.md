# WhatsApp Automation: The Secret Weapon

When a customer needs an electrician or plumber, they usually need one *now*. 

If they message you on WhatsApp and you don't reply within 5 minutes, they will message your competitor.

## The Problem with Manual Replies

You are busy under a sink or fixing a circuit panel. Your phone buzzes. You can't answer. By the time you get back to your truck, the customer has already booked someone else.

## The Solution: Intelligent WhatsApp Automation

With GrowthGrid's WhatsApp integration, we set up systems that act like your virtual receptionist.

- **Instant Acknowledgement:** "Hi! Thanks for reaching out to Acme Services. Are you experiencing an emergency?"
- **Automated Intake:** "Please describe the issue and send a photo if possible."
- **Smart Routing:** If they say it's an emergency, it bypasses the system and calls you directly. If it's routine, it sends them a link to your calendar to book an inspection.
