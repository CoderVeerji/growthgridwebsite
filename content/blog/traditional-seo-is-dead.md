# Why Traditional Local SEO is Dead

For years, local businesses could get away with simple SEO tactics. Stuffing the word "Plumber in New York" fifty times on a homepage used to work.

Not anymore.

Google's algorithms have evolved significantly, especially with the introduction of AI Overviews and the hyper-local focus of the Google Maps pack.

## The New Rules of Local Search

To dominate your local market today, you need more than just keywords. You need **entities** and **trust signals**.

1. **Google Business Profile Optimization:** Your GBP is more important than your website for local searches. It needs consistent updates, genuine reviews, and localized images.
2. **Review Velocity:** It's not just about having a 5-star rating. It's about how consistently you get new reviews. A steady stream of 4.8-star reviews beats an inactive profile with a perfect 5.0.
3. **Local Citations with Context:** Having your Name, Address, and Phone number (NAP) across the web is standard, but having them in contextually relevant directories (like industry-specific sites) moves the needle.
4. **Behavioral Signals:** If users search for your service, click your profile, and immediately hit "call" or "get directions", Google ranks you higher. If they bounce, you drop.
